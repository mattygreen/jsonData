Racial Discrimination

This collection includes news scripts about racial discrimination in housing, employment, immigration control legislation, and in mainstream political debates. Our research into when terms such as ‘racism’, ‘racialism’ come in and out of use can be revealing of how the language of race changes over time, and, in some cases, it can be suggestive of embedded ideas of what it means to be British. For example, when is the first time the word ‘racism’ is mentioned on the Home Service/R4 and in relation to what? Scroll down to find out.
